The flow into the lake is controlled by releases from the Madden Dam and uncontrolled rivers in the rest of the lake watershed. Flow out of the lake is to the Atlantic Ocean via the Gatun and Aqua Clara locks and Gatun Dam, and to the Pacific Ocean through the Pedro Miguel and Cocoli Locks. To set up the currents, set the total flow from the Madden Dam, the total flow to the Atlantic Ocean, and the total flow to the Pacific Ocean. You can choose a climatological estimate for each flow or enter your own value.
